Full feature list

Add new task
  • Tap the “+” button to open the new task screen.
  • Enter your task name and tap Save to quickly add it with default settings.
  • To set a specific date, time, or other details, tap the corresponding field to update it.
Recurring habits
  • On the task screen, tap Once to open repeat options. Tap again to close.
  • Tap the number to increase the frequncey. Press & drag it up or down to adjust it quickly.
  • Tap on "day" unit to switch the frequency between days, weeks, months, or years.
  • Tap Save to update your habit.
Edit task
  • In the list view, tap any task to open it for editing.
  • Update details as needed and tap Save to confirm your changes.
  • For recurring tasks, name changes apply to all instances. Other edits affect the selected and future occurrences only.
Quick add task
  • In the list view, press and hold a day label to instantly add a task for that date.
  • If you’ve scrolled away from today, tap the floating arrow button to jump back.
  • Press and hold the arrow button to drag it to your desired spot.
Calendar search
  • Tap the calendar icon on the bottom left to open your monthly view.
  • The calendar displays a heat map of your task load. The deeper the color, the more tasks you have for that day.
  • Tap a date to jump to the list view for that day.
  • Use the search bar to find any task and check its completion status at a glance.
App widget
  • Add the Dot It Down widget to your home screen to track daily progress at a glance.
  • The widget shows your remaining tasks for the day. A dynamic progress ring fills as you complete them.

FAQ

Do I need an account?
No. Everything stays on your device, so you can start using Dot It Down without creating an account.
How is it private and offline?
All data is stored locally on your phone. No login required, no data sent to servers. It works where you need it to: on planes, in subways, or even on a hike.
How no subscriptions or ads?
Dot It Down is a one-time purchase. Since it works fully offline, there are no ongoing server costs that need subscriptions or ads.
Any future features or updates?
Yes, intentionally updated to keep the app light, calm, and focused.
