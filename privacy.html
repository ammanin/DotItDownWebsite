Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Dot It Down ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Dot It Down mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

Dot It Down is designed to be a private-first application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

1.2 User-Generated Content

All tasks, schedules, and notes you create within the App are stored locally on your device. We have no technical means of accessing your data or viewing your content in any form.

1.3 Local Storage and Backups

  • Local Database: Your data is stored in a local database on your device.
  • Manual Backups: You may choose to export your data to a JSON file on your device's storage or a cloud service of your choice.
  • Automatic Backups: If enabled, the App may create local backup files on your device to prevent data loss.

We do not host, transmit, or manage these backups; all data remains fully under your control.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it for analytics, marketing, advertising, profiling, or any other purpose. The data you input is used solely to provide the App's core functionality (e.g., displaying your tasks, reminders, and notifications).

3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for your tasks.
  • Biometrics: To secure the App using your device's fingerprint or face recognition (if enabled by you).
  • Storage: To allow you to export and import your backups.

All permissions are optional and may be adjusted in your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: The App is distributed through the Google Play Store, which may collect certain information as governed by Google's own privacy policies.
  • In-App Purchases: Payments for premium features are processed securely through the Google Play Billing system. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment details.
  • Diagnostics: We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools. However, the Google Play Store may collect basic installation or crash data in accordance with its privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We do not store or retain any user data on external servers. All user-generated content and backups remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.

6. Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Optional biometric locking allows you to protect your local data from unauthorized access on your device. However, since your data is stored locally, security ultimately depends on the measures you apply to protect your device and backups.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children. Because no data is collected, the App complies with applicable children's privacy regulations such as COPPA.

8. Legal Compliance

Because we do not collect, process, or transmit personal information, we are exempt from several provisions of data protection laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Nevertheless, we uphold privacy-first principles and respect all user rights under these frameworks.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for updates.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please email us at contact@dotitdown.app.
